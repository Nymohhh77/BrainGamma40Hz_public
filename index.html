About BrainGamma40Hz

BrainGamma40Hz is a simple iOS application designed to support mental well-being through guided strobe light sessions. Inspired by research on brainwave entrainment, the app uses your device's flashlight to deliver a 40Hz flickering light pattern, which may promote relaxation and focus. Choose a session duration between 10 and 30 minutes and experience a calming light-based session.

How to Use BrainGamma40Hz

  1. Open the app on your iOS device.
  2. Select a session duration (10 to 30 minutes).
  3. Ensure your device's flashlight is enabled and place the device in a comfortable position.
  4. Start the session and relax as the flashlight strobes at 40Hz.
  5. For best results, use in a dimly lit room and focus gently on the light.
