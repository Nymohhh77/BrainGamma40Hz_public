About BrainGamma40Hz

BrainGamma40Hz is a simple iOS application designed to support mental well-being through guided strobe light sessions. Inspired by research on brainwave entrainment, the app uses your device's flashlight to deliver a 40Hz flickering light pattern, which may promote relaxation and focus. Choose a session duration between 10 and 30 minutes and experience a calming light-based session.

How to Use BrainGamma40Hz

  1. Open the app on your iPhone.
  2. Select a session duration (10 to 30 minutes).
  3. Ensure your device's flashlight is enabled and place the device in a comfortable position.
  4. Start the session and relax as the flashlight strobes at 40Hz.
  5. For best results, use in a dimly lit room and focus gently on the light.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Why does the app require flashlight access?

The app uses your device's flashlight to create the 40Hz strobe effect, which is central to the experience. Ensure flashlight permissions are enabled in your device settings.

What should I do if the flashlight doesn’t work?

- Check that flashlight permissions are enabled in Settings > BrainGamma40Hz.
- Ensure your device is not in low-power mode, as this may disable the flashlight.
- Restart the app or your device and try again.

Is the app safe for everyone?

The app includes a strobe light feature that may not be suitable for individuals with photosensitive epilepsy or other sensitivities to flashing lights. Please read the safety notice above and consult a doctor if unsure.

Can I adjust the strobe frequency?

Currently, the app is set to a fixed 40Hz frequency, based on research into brainwave entrainment. Future updates may include customizable settings.

The Science Behind BrainGamma40Hz

BrainGamma40Hz uses rhythmic light stimulation to entrain brain waves, potentially promoting relaxation and cognitive benefits. Users can adjust light intensity (10–100%) to balance effectiveness and comfort. Key concepts include:

  • Alpha Frequencies (8–12 Hz): Research suggests alpha stimulation promotes relaxed focus and meditative states, often inducing visual phenomena like phosphenes. Moderate intensity (20–50%) can reduce anxiety and enhance creativity [1].
  • Gamma Frequencies (30–40 Hz): Studies, such as those from MIT (2016–2019), indicate 40Hz gamma stimulation at higher intensity (50–100%) may enhance neural synchronization, potentially improving cognition and mood [2, 3].
  • Rhythmic Stimulation: Consistent, rhythmic light pulses are more effective at entraining brain waves compared to arrhythmic patterns, with intensity influencing neural response strength [4].

Safety Considerations

Approximately 3–5% of individuals with epilepsy are photosensitive and may be at risk from strobe lights. To minimize risks:

  • Adjust light intensity to a comfortable level (10–100%).
  • Use the app in a dark environment with eyes closed.
  • Position the device 10–20 cm from your eyes (e.g., on your chest while lying down or in front while sitting).
  • Consult a doctor if you have epilepsy, photosensitivity, or other medical conditions.
